Welcome to 2nd Adelaide Scout Group

We run a range of great programs for youth aged between 5 and 18, packed with fun, action, adventure, challenge and friends! Scouting offers activities in age based sections so you and or your child can have fun and adventure with others your own ages.

Adelaide 2nd Scout Group offers fun-filled programs for following sections:

By joining Adelaide 2nd Scout Group you are giving your children the opportunity for a fun, educational experience that forms a bridge between what they learn at home and at school. The Group is well staffed with skilled and trained leaders (but new Leaders are always welcome) who deliver great Scouting programs to the Sections.

Scouts incorporates nature, adventure, challenge, citizenship, leadership, values and great fun. It offers experience and challenge in a safe environment and fulfills a child's natural urges to learn and explore. It develops skills, self confidence, respect, and fosters attitudes that will benefit not only your children but the whole community.

We look forward to welcoming your family in person to Adelaide 2nd Scout Group.
